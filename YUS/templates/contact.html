Frequently Asked Questions

What types of construction projects does Istidama specialize in?

Istidama Construction specializes in a wide range of projects including residential buildings, commercial complexes, infrastructure development, and sustainable green buildings. We have extensive experience in both private and public sector projects across Saudi Arabia.

How can I request a quote for my construction project?

You can request a quote by filling out our contact form with details about your project, or by calling our office directly. For more complex projects, we recommend scheduling a consultation meeting with our project managers to discuss your specific requirements.

What makes Istidama different from other construction companies?

Our commitment to sustainability and innovation sets us apart. We incorporate eco-friendly materials and energy-efficient designs in all our projects. Additionally, our rigorous quality control processes and highly skilled team ensure exceptional results on every project, regardless of size.

How long does a typical construction project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope, size, and complexity. A small residential project might take 6-12 months, while large commercial developments could take 2-3 years. During our initial consultation, we'll provide you with a detailed timeline based on your specific project requirements.

Do you provide post-construction services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-construction services including maintenance, warranty services, and facility management. Our commitment to our clients extends well beyond project completion to ensure long-term satisfaction with our work.
