Our Story

Founded in 2010, Istidama Construction Company emerged from a vision to revolutionize the construction industry in Saudi Arabia. What began as a small team of dedicated professionals has grown into one of the region's most trusted construction partners.

Our name "Istidama" means sustainability in Arabic, reflecting our core commitment to environmental responsibility and long-lasting quality. We believe that every project we undertake should contribute positively to the community and environment.

Over the years, we have successfully completed hundreds of projects, ranging from residential complexes to large-scale commercial developments, always maintaining our standards of excellence and innovation.
